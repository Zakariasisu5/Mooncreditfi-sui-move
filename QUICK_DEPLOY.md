# Quick Deploy to Vercel - 5 Minutes ⚡

## Fastest Way to Deploy

### 1. Go to Vercel
Visit: https://vercel.com

### 2. Sign In
Click "Sign Up" → Sign in with GitHub

### 3. Import Repository
- Click "Add New..." → "Project"
- Select: `Zakariasisu5/Mooncreditfi-sui-move`
- Click "Import"

### 4. Deploy
- Framework: Vite (auto-detected)
- Click "Deploy"
- Wait 2-3 minutes ⏱️

### 5. Done! 🎉
Your app is live at: `https://mooncreditfi-sui-move.vercel.app`
